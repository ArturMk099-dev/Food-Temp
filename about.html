Our Story
Simple House was created with a passion for fresh food, quality
ingredients, and a friendly atmosphere. What started as a small
idea quickly became a place where people can enjoy delicious
pizzas, healthy salads, and flavorful noodles prepared
with care.
Every dish on our menu is made to bring comfort, great taste,
and memorable moments to our guests. We believe that good food
brings people together, which is why we work hard every day to
provide excellent service and unforgettable meals for
families and friends.
